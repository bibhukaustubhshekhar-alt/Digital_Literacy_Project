5 DO'S
DO keep your LinkedIn updated with your latest skills and projects.

DO use privacy settings to keep your private life separate from the public.

DO follow experts in your field to stay informed about job opportunities.

DO think twice before posting—if you wouldn't show it to a boss, don't post it.

DO fact-check news before you share it to keep your profile credible.

5 DON'TS
DON'T post "party" photos involving drinking or anything that looks unprofessional.

DON'T complain about professors or internships by name on public platforms.

DON'T argue with strangers in comment sections; it's a waste of time and energy.

DON'T share your exact location or personal IDs for your own safety.

DON'T ignore your tagged photos—untag yourself from anything embarrassing.
